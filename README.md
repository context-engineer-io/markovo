# Markovo

**Your AI Marketing Department**

Markovo is an AI-powered marketing platform that replaces traditional marketing agencies for SMBs. Manage your entire marketing strategy, content creation, SEO, ads, email, and analytics in just 5 minutes per day.

## Vision

Transform how small businesses approach marketing by automating 80% of agency work while augmenting the remaining 20% with intelligent AI assistance.

**Target:** Small-to-medium business owners currently paying $2,000-5,000/month to marketing agencies.

**Pricing:** $199-499/month (MVP)
